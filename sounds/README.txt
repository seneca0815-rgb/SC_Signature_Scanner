Place WAV files here:
  init.wav        - played on app startup
  activate.wav    - played when scanner is activated
  deactivate.wav  - played when scanner is deactivated
  signal.wav      - played when a signal is detected (default: off)

Recommended: 44.1 kHz, mono or stereo, short duration (< 3 seconds).
Generated with ElevenLabs or any WAV-compatible tool.

If WAV files are absent the app falls back to a system beep automatically.
Volume is controlled by the Windows volume mixer (Start > Settings >
System > Sound, or right-click the speaker icon in the taskbar).
