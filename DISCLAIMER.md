# Disclaimer

SC Signature Reader is a fan-made community tool for Star Citizen. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with Cloud Imperium Games (CIG) or Roberts Space Industries (RSI).

## ToS Compliance

We contacted CIG Player Support regarding this tool. Their response confirmed that CIG does not officially endorse third-party tools, and that tools violating the ToS — specifically those providing unfair advantages, reading game memory, or automating gameplay — are prohibited.

SC Signature Reader does none of these things:

- **Screen capture only** — no memory reading, no DLL injection, no modification of game files or processes
- **No unfair advantage** — signature values are publicly known and available to every player via community resources
- **No gameplay automation** — the player makes all decisions; the tool only displays information

We are confident SC Signature Reader complies with the Star Citizen Terms of Service. However, this assessment is ours, not CIG's. CIG has not issued an official stance on this specific tool.
